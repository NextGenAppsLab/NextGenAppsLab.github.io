Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our service

What is ContactScript?

ContactScript is a social network script you install on your own server to launch a public or private platform. You get full control over your data, design, and community rules.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! You can try ContactScript for free with full features. No credit card required.

Do I need a team to manage and grow my network?

Not necessarily. You can work with our trusted partners — experienced professionals and agencies who offer everything from setup to moderation, content creation, and growth. Check our Partners page for teams ready to run your network end-to-end.

How is ContactScript different from SaaS platforms?

ContactScript isn’t a cloud service with monthly fees. You can buy it once and own it forever. That means no outside control, no policy changes, and no hidden costs. Your data, your rules, your network — 100% yours.

Do I need a server or hosting to use ContactScript?

Yes, you’ll host the script on your own server or hosting provider. That gives you full independence and control.

Can I use ContactScript without tech skills?

The setup takes just 5 minutes. If you're not tech-savvy, you can hire a specialist or use a partner — no deep technical knowledge is needed.

How is ContactScript different from mainstream social media?

You own the entire platform. No censorship, no ads, no hidden algorithms, no data tracking. You're in charge of everything.

What plans are available and what's the difference?

Plans differ by duration:

- 6-month subscription with support & updates
- 1-year subscription with support & updates
- One-time purchase — own it forever, no recurring fees
All plans include full features.

Can I monetize my social network?

Absolutely. Subscriptions, paid access, ads — you decide how to earn. No middlemen.

Can I customize the design to fit my brand?

Yes. Use your own domain, logo, colors — make it truly yours.

Is my data and user data safe?

Yes. All data stays on your server — no third-party access. You’re in full control of security and privacy.

Do you offer an affiliate program?

Yes — earn 40% commission per sale by referring customers. We offer full support and premium partner visibility.
