Problems We Solve

Public social networks no longer work in your favor. We created a solution that frees you from their limitations:

Account blocks and deletions

Your account can be deleted at any time on public networks.

Our solution: You own the network — no one can block or delete your account.

Data leaks and collection

Your data is someone else's business.

Our solution: All data is stored with you. You decide who can see what.

Algorithms that don't serve you

Reach, filtering, moderation — you depend on someone else's decisions.
