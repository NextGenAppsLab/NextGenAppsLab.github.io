About Us

Next Gen Apps Lab is a company specializing in the development of innovative business applications. We create a platform for efficient interaction within business communities, knowledge sharing, and process automation using artificial intelligence.

Problem and Solution

Key Problem

Business communities are forced to use either traditional social networks (Facebook, LinkedIn), which are overloaded with irrelevant content, or corporate tools that lack flexibility in interaction.

Our Solution

We offer a smart AI-powered platform that automates processes, analyzes data, and helps in making strategic decisions.

Key Features

User Profiles

Create personalized profiles for companies and individual users.

Personalized Feeds

Subscribe to interesting profiles and receive relevant information.

Intelligent Support

AI analyzes discussions and provides insights.

Analytics and Forecasting

Analyze user behavior for strategic decision-making.

Market and Target Audience

Our platform is aimed at small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs), consulting firms, government and private organizations, as well as IT companies and startups.

Competitors and Advantages

Main Competitors

  • Slack, Microsoft Teams
  • Trello, Asana
  • Facebook Groups, LinkedIn Groups

Our Advantages

  • Narrow specialization in B2B communications
  • AI integration for decision automation
  • Built-in analytics and forecasting

Business Model

Premium Subscriptions

Advanced features: analytics, automation, personalized feeds.

Advertising and Partnerships

Targeted B2B advertising.

Technology Licensing

Sale of solutions to companies.

Financial Model

Expected Revenue

  • Year 1: $500K – $1M (subscriptions, small and medium companies).
  • Years 2–3: $2M – $5M (scaling, AI integration).
  • Year 3+: $10M+ (global expansion, technology licensing).

Required Investments

  • Stage 1: $500K – $1M (MVP development and testing).
  • Stage 2: $1.5M – $3M (scaling and AI integration).

Development Stages

Stage 1: Development and Launch (6–12 months)

  • MVP with basic features (profiles, feeds, discussions, messaging).
  • Testing with the first group of partners.
  • Feedback analysis and UX improvements.

Stage 2: AI Integration (12–18 months post-launch)

  • Development of intelligent content curation.
  • Automation of interaction processes.
  • Implementation of analytics and forecasting.

Risks and Mitigation

Main Risks

  • Low interest from the target audience in the early stages.
  • High competition from major players (Slack, Teams).
  • Technical challenges in AI integration.

Mitigation Strategies

  • Active engagement with the initial audience, collecting feedback.
  • Unique features and positioning as an AI-powered platform.
  • Thorough preparation and testing of AI before integration.
